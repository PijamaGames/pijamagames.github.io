Social Media Ways you can contact us

At Pijama Games, we are eager to hear you out on what you have to say. Because of that, we are providing you the following tools for you to express us your queries.

Use twitter if you want to talk about things related to our games. There you will find posts about upcoming games and current ones, where you can engage with the community. Go and check it out for yourself!

Instagram

On instagram, you can comment on our posts and send us DM's.
