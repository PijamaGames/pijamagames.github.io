Our newest 2D adventure Nelu Lotus Guardian

Discover the jungle with Nelu on this 2D adventure game where you will wonder through the amazon rainforest solving puzzles, making friends and fighting enemies.

This web browser game offers a single player experience and a two player online versus mode for those who like to battle between each other. And the best part is that it has mobile integration, making you able to play on the go on your smartphone

Play now for free!

Get a glimpse of our work with this teaser trailer, made thanks to our collaboration with Na agant, who composed this song for our game (The end).

Fight against enemies that want to harm your people, make friends with the wildlife of the jungle and explore the puzzles and secrets that the rainforest has to offer.

Climb the ranking on the 2 player competitive arena gamemode! You can choose between being Nelu and surviving for two full minutes or become the Game Master and spawn enemies strategically to avoid Nelu surviving the 2 minute mark. With 4 maps to choose from and giving you the option of selecting what lighting you want to play along, you will have lots of different combinations available.
