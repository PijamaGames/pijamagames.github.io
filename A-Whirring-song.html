Our first visual novel A whirring song

Embark your adventure as Johan, a starting mucisian that is trying to make a living by working at the jail as a security guard.

As days pass, you will be meeting new prisonners, learn about their past and solve challenges presented along your day. Will you invest time with the prisoners or choose to focus about your future carrer? Whatever you choose will be up to you.

MEET LUKA

Luka is a veteran guard that has been working in the jail for years. He has a lot of experience when handling the prisoners.

He will guide you through the ropes on your first day, making sure you understand how life at prison works.

Don't be late though, unless you want to be lectured about how those five minutes that you were late was half a robbery.

MEET FAY

Fay is your roomate who spends most of her time online playing games with her friends.

She plays in a team called Featherknights, but if she is not practising she might be throwing a party.

She cares about Johan and she tries to support him in reaching his dream as a musician.

MEET KALEV

Kalev is one of the inmates that you will find in the prison during your shift.

He doesn't talk to anybody and doesn't wan't to shower with other prisoners, because he doesn't like people watching his body.

Will you be able to get to know him better or will he give you the silent treatment.

MEET STANISLAV

Stanislav is one of those prisoners who can charm anyone.

He knows like the palm of his hand how life at jail goes and how to talk through trouble.

If you are looking for someone to talk to during your free time at your shift, Stanislav might be your man. Just avoid catching him in the middle of a "transaction".

Make choices during your adventure! You can see in real time how much an option has been chosen by other players. This might help you choose an option if in doubt, but it is recomended to make the decision you are more inclined to.
