    Nelu: Lotus Guardian A Whirring Song GemFever

Try out our new multiplayer game Gem Fever

Venture into the mines of Pillow Town where you will compete against up to 4 players. You can play locally if you are playing on a PC or online (in both platforms, PC and mobile).

This web browser game offers a multiplayer experience where your objective is to get as many gems as possible! Use your pickaxe to mine the ores and to hit other players. Players will get stunned and lose gems if hit. To avoid losing gems, you can deposit them inside the minecart.

Play now for free!

Spend your hard earned gems on the in-game store! You can buy faces and hats for your avatar to change your look in the mines. Go mining with style!

Customize your avatar that you will be using on your games. With different colors, skins, faces and hats you can make loads of different combinations. If you don't know what to wear, you can always use the randomize button!

There are some exclusive items that can only be obtained in events. Currently there is an event going on that started on the 13th of January until the 20th where if you login in the game you will be rewarded with our own logo's hat! Don't miss it!
